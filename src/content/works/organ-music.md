During Audium's inaugural residency, I composed and performed *ORGAN MUSIC*, a site-specific piece presented in total darkness over 24 live performances. The work explores the dissolution of form, imagining the body as unbounded and flowing. My throat, wired to transducers, became a resonator; speakers sympathetically click, snap, and throb to life; the entire building becomes a vibrating, encompassing mass.
