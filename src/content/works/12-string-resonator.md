A commissioned instrument built for a film score recording session with Yair Elazar Glotman at <a href="https://www.funkhaus-berlin.net/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Funkhaus Berlin</a>.

Twelve strings, tuned chromatically or in unison, resonate sympathetically when driven by an external audio source. At Funkhaus, a string quartet was routed through the resonator. Voices also sound particularly beautiful.

Under continual development — please get in touch for further information.
