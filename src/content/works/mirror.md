*Mirror* is a modified plate reverb instrument, modeled after the EMT-140. Six microphones around the gallery space were routed to voice coils that magnetized the surface of the plate without touching it, causing it to vibrate.

Four columns of empty staves are etched into the surface of the plate. The gesture of empty staves acts as a frame, bounding an indeterminate period of time and signifying ensuing mark-making as musical material. Here, the only marks are rust on the surface of the steel — the material becomes the score. As the room resonates through the steel, different perspectives and resonances are refracted and compressed into a music that re-composes the acoustics of the space it inhabits.
