Suspended Violin reconstructs the violin from fragments. Gestural figures and spatial trajectories evoke the various shapes of the instrument. The video spirals through a 300-million-point 3D model, capturing both precise detail and obscure digital artifacts. Recorded for fifth-order ambisonics.
