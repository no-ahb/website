A series of visual feedback portraits created by routing camera signals through chains of analog and digital processing. The camera feeds back on its own output, producing shifting, self-generating images that oscillate between figuration and abstraction.

Each portrait begins with a face — then the feedback takes over, dissolving features into fields of color and light that bear only a spectral resemblance to their source.
