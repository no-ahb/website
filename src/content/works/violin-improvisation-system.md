An electroacoustic violin system that integrates feedback, neural networks, and gestural tracking as tools for live improvisation. The system extends the violin into a real-time dialogue between performer, electronics, and acoustic space.

Gestural data from the bow and body are mapped to synthesis parameters, creating a responsive instrument that evolves with the performer's movements. Neural networks trained on improvisation data suggest harmonic and timbral pathways, while feedback loops blur the boundary between the acoustic violin and its electronic extensions.

The system is designed for live performance and is continually evolving.
