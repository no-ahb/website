Like removing a single note from a piano and placing it on the wall, *Interval* consists of variable length, wall-mounted, carefully tuned strings vibrating at resonant frequencies via self-oscillating feedback loops.

My solo show, *As Long as a Piece of String*, explored sound as a distinctly physical phenomenon: connective tissue that binds us to the spaces we inhabit. Five strings were placed around the gallery space such that their length, tension, and, consequently, their pitch were determined by the architectural and material makeup of the space. The strings resonated through the walls they were attached to. Each had a radically different sonic imprint, allowing the space itself to become the primary instrument.
